How to Use:

  • Input attacker's damage and pierce and defender's resist stats to calculate damage multipliers
  • Spell damage will be base damage times the relevent total multiplier (PvE or PvP)
  • Note that you must aaccount for unrounded pet stat values for the most precise results (the rounded values that display on the character stat page will give a very good approximation)
